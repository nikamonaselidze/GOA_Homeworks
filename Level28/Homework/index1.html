Khinkali

Khinkali Is a dumpling in Georgian cuisine. It is made of
twisted knobs of dough, stuffed with meat or vegetables and spices.

Khachapuri

Khachapuri is a traditional Georgian dish of cheese-filled bread. The bread is leavened and allowed to rise,
molded into various shapes, and then filled in the center with a mixture of cheese

Lobiani

Lobiani dish of bean-filled bread.
In Georgia the most popular is Rachuli Lobiani

Mwvadi

Mtsvadi is often prepared outdoors over an open fire. It is recommended to serve the dish with
tkemali, raw onion rings, sliced tomatoes, and pomegranate seeds.

Shawarma

Shawarma is thin slices of meat inside of bread, which is stacked
in an inverted cone, and roasted on a slow-turning vertical spit.
